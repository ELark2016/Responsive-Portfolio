About Me

Elizabeth Lark is a seasoned professional with several years of experience working in IT project delivery. She enjoys working with teams to help them achieve high performance. In addition to coaching teams, she also enjoys working with individuals to help them realize their professional development goals. As a mentor, she finds enjoyment in helping others realize their goals. Liz's educational background includes a B.S. in Computer Science. She is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), a certified Scrum Master (CSM) and a certified Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Practitioner. In addition to her project delivery certifications; she has also obtained a certificate in Full Stack Development. She enjoys working with teams to bring their customers ideas to life. Her extensive background in project delivery combined with her technical skills make her a well rounded professional in the world of technology.
